about us

The Wonagseged Coffee process begins at the source. Our coffee are chosen and established with great love and greater yet - consideration of origin, flavor profile and quality. All of these components help us to deliver consistent, execptional coffee with true approachability.

Wonagseged Coffee roasts our beans using the most eco-friendly roasting technology available. We have eliminated our carbon emissions by up to 80 percent in a smokeless roasting environment which increase overall flavor and quality.

Freshness is the key to reealing all of what coffee has to offer. Our beans are roasted per order and shipped directly to your door. We want our customers to experience coffee at its peak drinkability - just a few days after being roasted to perfection. Our process reaches completion when you truly taste a coffee's origin... and nothing extra.
